## Overview

Phicore is a mobile hearing testing company providing specialized occupational health services including Mobile Hearing Testing, Hearing Conservation Training, Noise Level Surveys, and Dosimetry Studies. As the sole developer on this project, I was brought on to design and build a professional web presence from the ground up, under a tight deadline with a hard launch date tied to an upcoming event.

The finished site gave Phicore a polished platform to showcase their services, accept client inquiries, and manage appointment scheduling. Resulting in three times the web traffic the business had seen before launch.

## Key Features

- **Appointment & Scheduling System**: Built a custom booking system using JavaScript and PHP, allowing clients to schedule mobile hearing tests and related services directly through the site.
- **Responsive Design**: Engineered the layout to be fully compatible with mobile devices across all screen sizes, ensuring a seamless experience for users on any device.
- **Service Showcase**: Designed dedicated sections for each of Phicore's core offerings — Mobile Hearing Testing, Hearing Conservation Training, Noise Level Surveys, and Dosimetry Studies — making it easy for potential clients to understand what they provide.
- **Performance-Focused Build**: Kept the stack lean with HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript to ensure fast load times and broad browser compatibility.

## Technologies Used

- **HTML5**: For semantic page structure and content.
- **CSS**: For styling, layout, and responsive behavior across screen sizes.
- **JavaScript**: For interactive elements and front-end scheduling logic.
- **PHP**: For back-end processing of the booking and scheduling system.

## Challenges and Learnings

The primary challenge was delivering a fully functional, polished product within a compressed timeline. I approached this by building a clear strategic plan early on, breaking the work into prioritized milestones, and maintaining close communication with the client throughout. Coordinating feedback and revisions efficiently while staying on schedule was a key part of making this project a success.

## Outcome

The site launched on time at [Phicore.com](https://www.phicore.com) and immediately exceeded expectations — delivering three times the web traffic the client had previously seen. The project demonstrated my ability to manage client relationships, work under pressure, and ship a complete product independently from design through deployment.
